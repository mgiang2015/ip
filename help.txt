HELP BOOKLET
1. Commands we have:
    list: List all your tasks
    done [task number]: Mark a task done
    delete [task number]: Delete a task from your computer
    help: Display help page
2. Kinds of tasks and syntax
    Todo:
        General plan for later.
        todo [task name]. Example: todo wash dishes
    Event:
        Task with a specific time, at a specific place.
        event [task name] /at [additional information]. Example: event meeting /at 5pm on Zoom
    Deadline:
        Task with a specific end date.
        deadline [task name] /by [YYYY.MM.DD] [HH:MM]. Example: deadline CS2103T /by 2020.10.13 23:59
